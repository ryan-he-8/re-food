Orange Glazed Chicken with Cheese Recipe:

Ingredients:
- 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
- 1 medium orange
- 1/2 cup grated cheese (your choice of cheese)
- 1/4 cup honey
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper

Instructions:
1. Preheat the oven to 375Â°F.
2. Zest the orange and juice it. Set aside.
3. Season the chicken pieces with salt and pepper.
4. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces and cook until browned on both sides.
5. Transfer the chicken to a baking dish.
6. In a small bowl, mix together the honey, orange juice, and orange zest.
7. Pour the orange glaze over the chicken.
8. Sprinkle the grated cheese over the chicken.
9. Cover the baking dish with foil.
10. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-40 minutes, until the chicken is cooked through.
11. Remove the foil and continue to bake for an additional 10-15 minutes or until the cheese is melted and golden.
12. Serve hot with your favorite side dish.

Mashed Potato and Lettuce Sandwich Recipe:
Ingredients:
- 1 large potato
- 1 leaf of lettuce
- 1 slice of bread
- 1 tablespoon of butter
- salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. Peel the potato and cut it into chunks. Boil in salted water until soft, about 15 minutes.
2. Drain the potatoes and mash them with a fork or a potato masher. Add butter and season with salt and pepper to taste.
3. Toast the bread slice.
4. Wash the lettuce leaf and pat it dry with a paper towel.
5. Spread the mashed potatoes on one side of the toast.
6. Place the lettuce leaf on top of the mashed potatoes.
7. Top with the other slice of bread.
8. Serve and enjoy.

Mixed Fruit Salad with Tomato Dressing Recipe:

Ingredients:
- 1 banana, sliced
- 1 apple, chopped
- 1 medium-sized tomato, diced
- Salt and black pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Instructions:
1. In a mixing bowl, combine the sliced banana and chopped apple. Set aside.
2. In another mixing bowl, whisk together the diced tomato, salt, black pepper, olive oil, honey, and lemon juice.
3. Pour the tomato dressing over the mixed fruit salad and gently toss until well combined.
4. Serve immediately and enjoy!
